About CareForBrains

The story behind the movement

Founded in May 2024, CareForBrains is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit building a compassionate world where mental health is understood, supported, and never stigmatized.

/ Our Story · N. 01

How it all began

CareForBrains was founded by two dedicated individuals with a shared mission to spread awareness about mental health and neurology. What started as a simple idea quickly grew into something much bigger.

From humble beginnings, we've expanded to offer a range of resources, workshops, and support networks aimed at reaching as many individuals and communities as possible. Our goal has always been to provide the tools and knowledge needed to make a lasting, positive impact on mental wellness.

Today, with 852+ volunteers across 22 chapters, CareForBrains continues to grow. We're committed to making mental health awareness a priority for the next generation and beyond.

/ Our Values · N. 02

What guides our work

At CareForBrains, our mission is to create a nurturing and inclusive environment. These core values drive everything we do.

Community

We provide a compassionate and supportive community for everyone. Connection is at the heart of mental well-being, and we build spaces where people feel seen and heard.

Education

We promote mental health education and raise awareness through accessible resources and programs. Knowledge is the first step toward understanding and healing.

Diversity

We embrace and support individuals from all backgrounds, ensuring inclusive mental health services and support that respect every identity and experience.

/ Our Journey · N. 03

Key milestones

From two people with a vision to a nationwide network of changemakers.

June 2024

501(c)(3) and PVSA certified

CareForBrains achieves official nonprofit status and becomes a certified organization for the President's Volunteer Service Award.

June 2024

CareForBrains is founded

Two passionate individuals launch the organization with a mission to spread mental health awareness and support through community engagement.

Summer 2024

Volunteer program launches

The first volunteer program goes live, offering PVSA-certified service hours to students across the country through multiple tracks.

Fall 2024

E-Card program launches

A new way to earn volunteer hours by creating digital cards of encouragement, spreading kindness and mental health support one card at a time.

2024 – 2025

22 chapters nationwide

School-based and community chapters launch across multiple states, creating local hubs for mental health education and outreach.

2025

STEM Internship launches

A year-round program connecting STEM education with mental health, teaching students how science and technology intersect with well-being.

2025

Podcast launches

3,000+ listeners across 4 continents. Real conversations about mental health, student life, and the people behind the mission.
