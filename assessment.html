Mental Health Assessment

Take a quick, confidential screening for depression or anxiety. These clinically validated tools can help you understand how you're feeling and guide your next steps.

⚠

Important: These screenings are not a diagnosis. They are validated tools used by healthcare professionals to assess symptom severity. Only a licensed provider can diagnose a mental health condition. If you are in crisis, call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or text HELLO to 741741 (Crisis Text Line).
