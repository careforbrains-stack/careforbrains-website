Why start a chapter?

Starting a CareForBrains chapter means becoming a mental health leader in your school or community. Whether you're passionate about advocacy, connection, or change — your chapter helps spark important conversations.

Raise Awareness

Start meaningful conversations about mental health and reduce stigma in your school or community.

Promote Our Programs

Share wellness quizzes, podcasts, and e-cards created by youth, for youth.

Support Others

Build a safe space where your peers feel heard, understood, and uplifted.

Create Change

Plan events, host initiatives, and partner with teachers or clubs to reach more people.
