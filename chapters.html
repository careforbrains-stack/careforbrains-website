/ Chapter Types · N. 04

Two ways to lead

Whether at your school or in your neighborhood, there's a chapter model for you.

School Chapter

Operate within your school as an official club. Host events, workshops, and awareness campaigns on campus.

  • Faculty advisor required
  • Regular meetings during school year
  • Access to school event spaces
  • Perfect for high school students

Community Chapter

Serve your wider community beyond school walls. Partner with local organizations and reach more people.

  • Open to all ages and backgrounds
  • Year-round programming
  • Partner with local nonprofits
  • Greater reach and flexibility
/ Start a Chapter · N. 05

How to get started

Four steps to bring CareForBrains to your community.

01

Apply

Fill out the chapter application form with details about your school or community and your vision for the chapter.

02

Interview

Chat with our team about your plans, goals, and how we can support you. We'll make sure it's a great fit.

03

Onboard

Receive your chapter toolkit, branding guidelines, training materials, and access to the CareForBrains resource hub.

04

Launch

Host your first event, recruit members, and start making an impact. Our national team is with you every step.

Apply to start a CareForBrains chapter in your school or community. We provide everything you need to get started.
