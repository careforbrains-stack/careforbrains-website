/ Apply · N. 03

Start a chapter.

Passionate about mental health? Start a CareForBrains chapter in your school or community.

Age Requirement

Must be 13+ years old to start or lead a chapter.

Time Commitment

Commit to at least 2 hours per week for chapter activities.

Space Access

Have access to a school or community space for meetings and events.

Steps to apply

01

Submit your application

Fill out the chapter application form with your details and vision for your chapter.

02

Interview with our team

Meet with the CareForBrains leadership team to discuss your goals and commitment.

03

Complete orientation

Complete chapter leader orientation to learn our methods, branding, and best practices.

04

Launch your chapter

Launch your chapter with full support from the CareForBrains network.

/ Benefits · N. 04

What you get.

Brand & Materials

Official CareForBrains branding, templates, and marketing materials to represent the organization.

Training

Comprehensive training for chapter leaders and volunteers on mental health awareness and event planning.

PVSA Certification

Your volunteers earn nationally recognized Presidential Volunteer Service Award hours.

Network

Connect with 22+ chapters and 852+ volunteers nationwide for collaboration and shared learning.

Support

Dedicated mentorship from the CareForBrains leadership team to guide your chapter's growth.

Events

Access to national events, campaigns, and collaboration opportunities across all chapters.
