/ Process · N. 01

How it works

1

Pick a template

Choose a style that fits the message. Uplifting, kind, or funny. Keep it supportive and respectful.

2

Write your note

Add a short message of support. One or two sentences is perfect. Be kind, specific, and encouraging.

3

Earn verified hours

Submit your card and earn PVSA-certified volunteer hours. Verified service recognized by a presidential program. Every card counts.

/ Guidelines · N. 02

Quick rules

PVSA-Certified Hours

One e-card equals 30 minutes of PVSA-certified volunteer credit. These hours are officially verified and recognized through the President's Volunteer Service Award program. Create cards on any platform like Canva.

  • At least 5 words of writing on each card
  • Content should relate to mental health and be appropriate for teens
  • Be kind and inclusive, no discriminatory or rude content
  • Text must be easy to read
  • Do not use AI-generated or bulk-created cards
