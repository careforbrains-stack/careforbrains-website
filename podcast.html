/ About the Show

Real conversations about mental health

The CareForBrains Podcast brings honest, unfiltered conversations about mental health to listeners across 4 continents. We talk with students, counselors, researchers, and advocates about the topics that matter most to young people today.

No scripts, no sugarcoating. Just real stories from real people navigating mental health in a world that does not always make it easy. Whether you are dealing with anxiety, looking for coping strategies, or want to understand neurodiversity, there is an episode for you.
