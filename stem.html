/ Program · N. 01

STEM meets mental health

At CareForBrains we are dedicated to our mission of promoting mental well-being and furthering the knowledge of our members. In line with these goals we have created CareForBrains: STEM Exploration — a program where you will learn about the basics of STEM and how they connect to mental health.

/ Curriculum · N. 02

What you will learn

Four pillars of STEM, each explored through the lens of mental health and well-being.

Science

Brain Chemistry & Function

Students investigate how stress, mindfulness, and happiness affect brain chemistry and function, building a strong foundation in the science of mental health.

Technology

AI & Cybersecurity

Students explore how AI and cybersecurity work, and how these tools are increasingly used to monitor, protect, and improve mental well-being.

Engineering

Design & 3D Modeling

Students learn sketching and 3D modeling with Fusion 360, using design thinking to create innovations that could support mental health.

Mathematics

Patterns & Reasoning

Students discover how patterns, probability, and mathematical reasoning help bring clarity to mental health issues and emotional complexity.
