New Program

STEM Exploration

A year-round program connecting STEM education with mental health. Launching June 2025.

/ Program · N. 01

What is STEM Exploration?

STEM Exploration is CareForBrains' newest initiative — a year-round program that teaches students how science, technology, engineering, and math intersect with mental health and well-being. Through hands-on projects, workshops, and mentorship, students explore the science behind the mind.

Neuroscience

Understanding how the brain works, neural pathways, and the biology of mental health.

Technology

How tech is used in mental health — apps, telehealth, AI-assisted therapy, and digital wellness tools.

Research

Learning research methodology, data analysis, and contributing to real mental health studies.

Engineering

Designing solutions — from wearable wellness devices to accessible mental health platforms.

/ Structure · N. 02

How it's structured.

A year-round program organized into seasonal quarters, each building on the last.

Fall
Foundations
Introduction to STEM + mental health, team formation, and project ideation. Build the groundwork for the year ahead.
Winter
Deep Dive
Focused workshops, guest speakers from the field, and hands-on experiments. Go deep into the areas that interest you most.
Spring
Build
Students develop their own STEM x mental health projects with mentorship from professionals in the field.
Summer
Showcase
Present projects, attend the CareForBrains STEM Summit, and earn certificates. Celebrate what you built.
/ Eligibility · N. 03

Who it's for.

STEM Exploration is open to curious minds everywhere.

  • Open to students ages 13 – 22
  • No prior STEM experience needed
  • Interest in mental health, science, or making a difference
  • Available virtually — participate from anywhere
/ Benefits · N. 04

Program benefits.

Everything you gain when you join STEM Exploration.

/01

Mentorship

1-on-1 mentorship from STEM professionals and mental health experts who guide your journey.

/02

Certificate

Official CareForBrains STEM certificate upon completion of the full program year.

/03

Volunteer Hours

Earn PVSA-eligible volunteer hours throughout the program for your service record.

/04

Portfolio

Build real projects for your college applications and resume that showcase your impact.

/05

Network

Connect with like-minded students across the country who share your passion for change.

/06

Letters of Rec

Top participants receive letters of recommendation from program leads and mentors.
