New Program

STEM meets mental health

A year-round program exploring how science, technology, engineering, and math intersect with mental health and neuroscience.

Launching June 2025
/ The Program · N. 01

What is STEM Exploration?

STEM Exploration is CareForBrains' newest initiative, designed to bridge the gap between STEM education and mental health awareness. Students learn how neuroscience, data science, and technology are shaping the future of mental healthcare.

Through interactive workshops, guest speakers, hands-on projects, and collaborative research, participants gain a unique understanding of the science behind mental health while building real STEM skills.

The program runs year-round with monthly sessions, making it easy to participate alongside school and other commitments.

Science + Wellness

Where neuroscience meets mental health advocacy

/ Focus Areas · N. 02

What you'll explore

Each month dives into a different intersection of STEM and mental health.

Neuroscience

Explore brain structure, neural pathways, and the biological foundations of emotions, stress, and mental health conditions.

Digital Health

Study how apps, AI, and wearable tech are transforming mental health screening, treatment, and support systems.

Data & Research

Learn how data science and research methodology drive breakthroughs in understanding and treating mental health.

/ Program Structure · N. 03

How the program works

A structured but flexible format that fits around your schedule.

Monthly Sessions

Interactive virtual workshops

Each month features a live session led by STEM professionals, researchers, or mental health experts exploring a different topic at the intersection of science and wellness.

Hands-On Projects

Apply what you learn

Between sessions, work on guided projects that put concepts into practice. Build a mental health app prototype, analyze research data, or design a neuroscience experiment.

Guest Speakers

Learn from the field

Hear from neuroscientists, psychologists, health-tech founders, and researchers who are actively working at the intersection of STEM and mental health.

Capstone

Showcase your work

At the end of each cycle, present your project to peers and mentors. Outstanding projects may be featured on the CareForBrains platform.

/ Why Join · N. 04

What you'll gain

Beyond knowledge, the program opens doors to meaningful experience and impact.

PVSA volunteer hours

All program participation counts toward your PVSA-certified volunteer hours with CareForBrains.

College-ready portfolio

Build a portfolio of STEM projects with real-world impact that stands out on college applications.

Expert mentorship

Connect directly with STEM professionals and researchers who can guide your learning and career path.

Certificate of completion

Earn a CareForBrains STEM Exploration certificate recognizing your participation and achievements.
