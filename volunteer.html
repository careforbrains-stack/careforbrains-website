/ Training Modules · N. 02

What you'll learn

Our volunteer program is a structured training course. Complete modules, build real skills, and earn hours along the way.

M1

Mental Health Foundations

Understanding mental health conditions, the science of well-being, stigma reduction, and the language of compassionate support.

M2

Community Outreach

Learn how to plan awareness campaigns, host workshops, create educational content, and engage your local community effectively.

M3

Leadership & Advocacy

Develop skills in event management, public speaking, team leadership, and advocating for mental health resources and policy.

/ Roles · N. 03

Ways to contribute

Choose a track that matches your strengths. Every role earns PVSA hours.

Content Creator

Write blog posts, design social media graphics, produce educational materials, and create awareness content that reaches thousands.

Event Organizer

Plan and run mental health workshops, awareness events, fundraisers, and community engagement activities at your chapter.

Peer Educator

Lead training sessions and workshops for other volunteers and community members. Teach mental health literacy and coping strategies.

Tech & Research

Help with web development, data analysis, research projects, and building digital tools that support the mission.

/ Recognition · N. 04

PVSA certified hours

CareForBrains is a certified organization for the President's Volunteer Service Award. Every hour you volunteer with us counts toward earning a nationally recognized award signed by the President of the United States.

Whether you're looking to strengthen your college application, build leadership experience, or simply give back, PVSA certification ensures your service is officially recognized.

Presidential Recognition

Official PVSA certification for all volunteer hours logged with CareForBrains.
